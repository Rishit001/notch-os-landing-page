Questions Before You Book

If something's stopping you from booking a call, the answer is probably here.

Is this just another ChatGPT wrapper?

No. NOTCH OS is a multi-agent operating system where each agent is purpose-built for a specific business function — trained on your brand, SOPs, and client types. It's not a chat interface. It's a system that runs workflows, generates deliverables, and executes tasks without you prompting it every time.

How long until it's actually useful?

Most founders see output within 48 hours of onboarding. The strategy call takes ~90 minutes, agents get configured same day, and by day 2 content is being generated, follow-ups are running, and research reports are landing in your inbox.

What does the "book a call" actually involve?

A 30-minute discovery call where we learn your business, identify your highest-leverage agents, and make sure NOTCH OS is the right fit. No hard selling. If it's not right for you, we'll tell you.

How do you keep my business data secure?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Agent memory is sandboxed per account. We don't train models on your business data, and we never share outputs or inputs between clients.

Can I white-label it for my agency clients?

Yes — white-label branding is available exclusively on the Enterprise plan. You can run NOTCH OS under your own brand name and deploy it for up to 8 clients with a dedicated onboarding engineer.

How long does the setup take?

48 hours from your strategy call to a fully running system. The Done-With-You add-on covers configuration, voice training, SOP setup, and a 30-day check-in to make sure everything's running right.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel any time, no lock-in. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee — if NOTCH OS doesn't save you more than it costs in the first 30 days, you get a full refund.
